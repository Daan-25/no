Chronology

Methodology

Design principles behind this archive

Verification first

  • Separate confirmed records from unresolved claims.
  • Anchor timeline entries to date-stamped source categories.
  • Update language when court status changes.

Information architecture

  • Readers should understand the sequence in under five minutes.
  • Every section should lead back to source material.
  • Visual drama should never overpower factual clarity.
